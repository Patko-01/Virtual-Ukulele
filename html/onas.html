The story of Virtual Ukulele

First things first, this project is my graduation project, but that's not the whole story. I started the development of this project a year ago (in 2021). At the time, I was really into music, I have played the ukulele for about 4 years and was starting with electric guitar. The idea of a Virtual music tool has been growing inside my head, and of course, I chose the ukulele as the tool... I didn't know much about javascript, which was quite necessary for this project, I studied computer science and I knew to program, but not in javascript. So I started studying javascript, day after day... After one year of learning javascript on this project, I was able to finish my own Virtual music tool... This is Virtual Ukulele.
